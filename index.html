Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset



Welcome to our belly button biodiversity page. Improbable Beef is in search of bacteria candidate to produce synthetic meat and had volunteer swabbing their belly button for science. Here, you will find information about the bacterial flora colonizing the navel of each donor. Use the dropdown menu to select a subject ID and the demographic information will be displayed for each volunteer. The Top 10 bacterial species will be displayed on the bar graph while the whole flora can be visualized on the bubble chart. Both charts are interactive with scientific names appearing when hoovering the cursor over the data. The gauge chart shows the belly button washing frequency for the selected IDs. Enjoy exploring the world of bacteria.
